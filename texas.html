OVERVIEW
I was in San Angelo for around 6 months in training for the Army. Although most of my time was at work, I still had some weekends to travel around Texas!
LOCAL CULTURE
You will know as soon as you step foot in Texas, whether from the extreme heat or the high speed limit. It is a true western town and is exactly how people outside of Texas view Texas. I was in Texas from March-September, so I experienced the summer heat. Many days were over 100° and it rarely rained.
ATTRACTIONS
There is not much to do in the small town of San Angelo, Texas, but you can still find something you like. You can easily travel to San Antonio, Texas and go to Six Flags, or explore more of downtown San Angelo if you decide to stay local.

FOOD
The best thing about Texas is easily its food, and it is the same for this small town. My absolute favorite thing about San Angelo is this hibachi restaurant called Bonsai Garden, I still can’t find one that compares! There is also a plethora of Tex-Mex and barbecue, but you can truly find any restaurant that fits your taste.
