CULTURE
Japan culture is very modest and kind. They are very conservative with clothing, especially in smaller cities so it is something to keep in mind when packing. There are many customs and traditions that you will need to be prepared for if you are from Western countries. Also, it is culturally correct to be polite, which is another reason everyone is extraordinarily nice!
